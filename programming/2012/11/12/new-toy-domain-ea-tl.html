New toy domain; ea.tl

12 Nov 2012

My new toy domain ea.tl is now working and my beloved old domain just does a permanent redirect to the new host from now on. Fancy shit, eh ? I know. I'm curious how soon Google will realize this new situation and update its indices. With this new very short vanity domain, I also set up a new email for myself...

To do the permanent redirect from my old domain to new one, Apache HTTPD has this awesome feature, check this out:

<VirtualHost *:80>
        ServerName www.bzzzt.biz
        ServerAlias bzzzt.biz *.bzzzt.biz
        Redirect permanent / http://ea.tl/
</VirtualHost>

Mind the trailing /, at the end of ea.tl, that matters.

Cheers... I'm just getting more awesome day by day.
