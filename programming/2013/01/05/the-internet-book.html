The Internet Book

05 Jan 2013

I had this idea of making an animated and narrated documentary of how internet (word wide web) works. It's a phenomenon we use daily, yet, very few people appreciate what's going on during that few seconds while a page is being retrieved from a server thousands of kilometers away.

I had to start from somewhere, so, I start writing a mini book about how world wide web works.

Please feel free to contribute.
